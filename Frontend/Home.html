Our History

Paris Sweet Patisserie was founded in 2011 with the goal of bringing authentic French pastries to the heart of the city. Our journey began with a small bakery in Paris, and now we are proud to share our passion for fine pastries with the world. Every item we create is made with the finest ingredients, love, and dedication to the art of baking.

From croissants to custom cakes, we aim to offer an unforgettable experience with every bite. Join us on this sweet journey and experience the taste of Paris!

Our Team

Head Pastry Chef

Camile Nguyen is a pastry chef with over 45 years of experience, specialized in mousse cake, birthday cake, and custom wedding cake. He was born in Toulouse, France, to a French father and a Vietnamese mother, both of whom were also bakers. His family used to own a bakery in Paris, where he developed his love for French pastries from a young age. In 2005, he moved to the U.S. and opened his first bakery, Le Bec Fin, with his brother, earning praise for their authentic French creations. In 2008, Camile had the honor of serving as the pastry chef for President George W. Bush Jr. He later opened Paris Sweet in 2011, bringing a taste of Paris to Houston through his delicious pastries.
